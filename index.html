Who We Are

Twin sisters,
one love of wine

We're identical twins who discovered early on that we shared more than just a face — we share a deep, unstoppable passion for wine. Together we've made it our mission to bring that love to everyone around us: making the world of wine feel warm, welcoming, and genuinely exciting.

Whether you're curious about your first Burgundy or looking to deepen what you already know, we're here to guide the journey. From the rolling vineyards of Bordeaux to the sun-drenched slopes of Sicily, we share stories, tasting notes, and the sheer joy of discovery — one glass at a time.

Wine Tasting Evenings

Our tasting evenings are crafted to be as much about atmosphere as they are about wine. Guided warmly by the two of us, each evening takes you on a journey through carefully chosen bottles — telling the story behind every glass, from the vineyard to your table.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, planning a unique date night, or simply gathering friends for something a little different, we tailor each evening to your group.

  • Guided by your hosts — us, the Uncorked Duo
  • Curated wine selections from around the world
  • Small, intimate group settings
  • Perfect for celebrations, hen parties & private events
  • Suitable for all levels — beginners very welcome
