Who We Are

Twin sisters,
one love of wine

We're Uncorked Duo – two sisters exploring the wine world one glass (read: bottle) at a time. Our interest in wine started early, through travel with our family. From visiting vineyards to experiencing different food cultures and local wines, we were introduced to the idea that wine is about much more than what's in the glass.

Since then, we've carried on that tradition in our own way – travelling to wine regions, attending tastings, and visiting wine festivals around the world. Along the way, we've developed a deeper appreciation not just for wine itself, but for the stories, people, and cultures behind it. For us, wine isn't just a drink or a hobby. It's a way of understanding a place, a moment, and a shared experience.

We use our platform to document what we're learning – from wine education and tastings to travel, events, and unexpected pairings. More recently, we've started hosting our own tastings, creating relaxed and engaging spaces for others to explore wine in a way that feels accessible and enjoyable. From France, Italy, and Spain to Turkey, Japan, and beyond, we share stories, tasting notes, and the sheer joy of discovery, one bottle at a time.

We're Uncorked Duo – two sisters building a platform around wine, travel, and tastings, with a background in hospitality, communications, and events, and WSET Level 2 (Distinction).

We're currently growing our presence in the wine space and are keen to collaborate with brands, venues, and organisations on thoughtful, engaging content and experiences.

  • Wine travel & destination features We document wine regions, vineyards, and wine-themed festivals, focusing on both the wines and the wider cultural experience.

01

Wine Travel & Tourism

We explore wine regions, vineyards, and festivals around the world, sharing the stories, people, and cultures behind the wines. From established regions to lesser-known destinations, we focus on experiences that bring wine to life.

Wine travel, for us, is about far more than what's in the glass — it's about understanding a place, its people, and the history behind every bottle.

  • Visits to vineyards, estates, and wine-producing regions
  • Wine festivals and cultural experiences worldwide
  • Tasting notes and destination guides
  • From France, Italy, and Spain to Turkey, Japan, and beyond

02

Wine Education

We break down wine in a way that is clear, engaging, and approachable. With a WSET Level 2 qualification (Distinction) and Level 3 in progress, we share what we're learning through content that helps make sense of regions, grape varieties, styles, and pairings — without unnecessary jargon.

Want us to bring our education in person? We'd love to hear about what you have in mind — from corporate team events to private masterclasses.

  • Wine regions, grape varieties & tasting notes demystified
  • Food & wine pairing guides
  • Regular content on Instagram
  • Private & corporate masterclasses available on request
  • No prior wine knowledge needed

03

Wine Tastings

We host relaxed, interactive wine tastings designed to make wine more accessible and enjoyable. Whether it's themed tastings, food and wine pairings, or private events, our focus is on creating engaging experiences that encourage curiosity and discovery.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, planning a unique date night, or simply gathering friends for something a little different, we tailor each evening to your group.

  • Guided by your hosts — us, the Uncorked Duo
  • Curated wine selections from around the world
  • Small, intimate group settings
  • Perfect for celebrations, hen parties & private events
  • Suitable for all levels — beginners very welcome
